Palindrome Checker

According to Cambridge Dictionary, a palindrome is a word or group of words that is the same when you read it forward from the beginning or backward from the end.

My palindrome checker allows users to enter text or numbers without punctuation, case, or space and check for palindromes by clicking the "Check" button.

Features:

  • Case Insensitivity: Ignores letter case.
  • Punctuation and Space Handling: Automatically removes punctuation and spaces.
  • Numeric Palindromes: Supports checking numbers like “12321”.
